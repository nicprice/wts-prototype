Report a UK waste movement, export or import

Movers, exporters and importers of hazardous, non-hazardous and green list waste must use the service to notify the UK’s environmental regulatory bodies.

You can use this service to:

  • enter the details of a UK waste movement, export or import
  • update any estimates you may have entered – for example, collection date, first carrier or waste quantity
  • download and print an Annex VII form to include with a green list waste export, using information you’ve provided
  • download and print a waste transfer note or waste information note, using information you’ve provided
Start now

Before you start

Before you can use the Report a UK waste movement, export or import service, you must login or create a Government Gateway and Defra account. You can set up your accounts when you select ‘start now’.

You'll need to pay your annual service charge if you’re accessing the service for the first time.

If you’re an agent or broker acting on behalf of a business or organisation, you must use your own business details.:

Government Gateway

Sign in or create a Government Gateway account using your business or organisation email. For example, name@company.com.

Do not use a Government Gateway account created to access your personal information.

Defra account

Sign in or create a Defra account for your business or organisation.

If you're the first person from your business or organisation to create a Defra account, you’ll be setting up the account for your business or organisation.

You’ll be automatically made the admin and will be able to add team members.

Export and import controls

If you plan to export waste from the UK, you must report this at least 3 working days before the waste is picked up by the first carrier. You’ll be able to update estimates to actuals later.

If you plan to export waste from the UK, you must report this at least 3 working days before the waste is picked up by the first carrier. You’ll be able to update estimates to actuals later.

Get help

You can contact the service support team if you need help with a technical issue, such as problems accessing the service.

The move and track waste service support team will not be able to answer regulatory questions.

If you need help from a regulator to complete export or import notifications, you can contact your national regulatory team.

Your national regulatory team will not be able to answer technical questions about service problems.
